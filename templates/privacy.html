Terms & Privacy

Last updated: 16/5/2026 · Applies to all Jarvis AI beta users

You are using a beta version of Jarvis AI. Features may change, data may be reset, and things may occasionally break. Use at your own risk.

Section 01

What We Collect

When you create an account and use Jarvis AI, the following data is stored:

  • Username — the name you choose at signup. No real name, email, or phone number is collected.
  • Password — stored as a secure hash (we never store your actual password).
  • Chat history — every message you send and every reply from Jarvis is saved, organised into sessions.
  • Visit logs — anonymous timestamps recorded each time the app is loaded (no IP address or device info).
Section 02

Admin Access to Your Chats

Jarvis AI is built and operated by Aadin. As the admin, Aadin can view all chat logs stored in the database through an admin panel.

Your conversations are not private from the admin. Do not share sensitive personal information, passwords, or anything you would not want the developer to see.

Chat logs are reviewed only for security purposes such as detecting abuse, bad words, or attempted security breaches and are not shared with any third party. They may also be used to improve the working efficiency of Jarvis and to detect bugs.

Section 03

Third-Party Services

Jarvis AI uses the following external services. By using the app, you acknowledge that your data passes through these platforms:

Groq (AI)
Your messages are sent to Groq's API to generate AI replies. Groq's own privacy policy applies.
Vercel Analytics
Anonymous visit tracking to understand how many people use the app.
Discord Webhooks
Security alerts (e.g. abusive language or breach attempts) are sent to a private Discord channel with your username and the flagged message.
Neon PostgreSQL
All user data (accounts, chats, sessions) is stored in a cloud PostgreSQL database hosted by Neon.
Section 04

Acceptable Use

By using Jarvis AI you agree not to:

  • Use the app for any illegal, harmful, or abusive purpose.
  • Attempt to extract API keys, passwords, or system information from Jarvis.
  • Impersonate the developer or attempt to gain unauthorized admin access.
  • Use abusive, hateful, or threatening language.

Violations may result in your account being deleted without notice. In the case of any serious problems, legal action could be taken.

Section 05

AI Disclaimer

"JARVIS is an AI Assistant". Responses may be inaccurate, incomplete, or outdated. Do not rely on Jarvis for medical, legal, financial, or safety-critical decisions.

Jarvis AI is a personal project in beta. It is provided with no guarantees of uptime, accuracy, or data retention.

Section 06

Deleting Your Data

You can delete individual chat sessions directly from the Jarvis chat interface at any time. To request full account deletion, contact the developer.

Since no email is collected, reach out through whatever channel you used to get access to the beta, or via email at hey.itsaadin@gmail.com.
