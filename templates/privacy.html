Terms & Privacy

Last updated: 17/5/2026 · Applies to all Jarvis AI beta users

You are using a beta version of Jarvis AI. Features may change, data may be reset, and things may occasionally break. Use at your own risk.

Section 01

What We Collect

When you create an account and use Jarvis AI, the following data is stored:

  • UsernameThe name you choose at signup. No real name, email, or phone number is collected.
  • PasswordStored as a secure hash — we never store your actual password.
  • Chat historyEvery message you send and every reply from Jarvis is saved, organised into sessions.
  • Calculation historyMath expressions you calculate are saved and can be reviewed from the chat interface.
  • Visit logsAnonymous timestamps recorded each time the app is loaded. No IP address or device info is stored.
  • Session activityYour last active time (heartbeat) is periodically recorded to track active users in the admin panel.
  • PDF content (temporary)When using the Quiz or Exam features, your uploaded PDF text is extracted and sent to Groq's API. It is not stored permanently on our servers.
  • Image promptsWhen you generate an image, the text prompt is saved in your chat history like any other message.
Section 02

Features & How They Work

Jarvis AI includes the following features. Here's what each one does with your data:

AI Chat
Your messages are sent to Groq's API (LLaMA model) to generate replies. Chat history is saved to the database and visible to the admin.
AI Image Generation
Your prompt is sent to Pollinations.ai to generate an image. The prompt is stored in chat history. The image itself is not stored on our servers — only the URL.
Quiz Generator
You upload a PDF — its text is extracted and sent to Groq's API to generate multiple-choice questions. The PDF is not stored permanently on our servers.
Model Exam Generator
Similar to the Quiz Generator — your PDF text is sent to Groq to produce a structured exam paper with questions, options, answers, and explanations.
Calculator
Math expressions are evaluated securely and saved to your calculation history in the database.
Time & Date
Current time and date are read from the server (IST timezone) and returned to you. Nothing additional is stored.
Section 03

Admin Access to Your Data

Jarvis AI is built and operated by Aadin. As the admin, Aadin can view all chat logs, usernames, visit counts, and message counts through a password-protected admin panel.

Your conversations are not private from the admin. Do not share sensitive personal information, passwords, or anything you would not want the developer to see.

Chat logs are reviewed only for security purposes such as detecting abuse, bad words, or attempted security breaches and are not shared with any third party. They may also be used to improve the working efficiency of Jarvis and to detect bugs.

Security alerts: If you use abusive language or attempt to extract system secrets, an automated alert including your username and the flagged message is sent to a private Discord channel.

Section 04

Sessions & Login Persistence

When you log in, your session is remembered for 30 days. You will stay logged in across browser restarts without needing to sign in again, until you manually log out or the session expires.

Your last active time (heartbeat) is periodically recorded while you use the app. This helps track active user counts in the admin panel.

Section 05

Third-Party Services

Jarvis AI uses the following external services. By using the app, you acknowledge that your data passes through these platforms:

Groq (AI — Chat, Quiz & Exam)
Your chat messages and PDF text content are sent to Groq's API to generate AI replies, quiz questions, and exam papers. Groq's own privacy policy applies.
Pollinations.ai (Image Generation)
Your image prompts are sent to Pollinations.ai to generate images. No account or API key is required. Their service is free and open. Their own terms apply.
Vercel Analytics
Anonymous visit tracking to understand how many people use the app.
Discord Webhooks
Security alerts (abusive language, breach attempts) are sent to a private Discord channel with your username and the flagged message.
Neon PostgreSQL
All user data (accounts, chats, sessions, calculation history) is stored in a cloud PostgreSQL database hosted by Neon.
