BETA PROGRAM

Meet Jarvis.

Jarvis is your personal AI assistant — built to chat, create, learn, and help you get things done. This page explains honestly how the app works and how your data is handled. No jargon, no surprises.

Overview

Privacy & Transparency

No email, no phone number, no real name — just a username and a password. No ads, no selling your data to anyone. Jarvis was built to be genuinely useful, not to harvest anything from you.

Your chats are saved so Jarvis can remember context and you can go back to them. That's basically why any data exists. The rest of this page just walks through how each feature works so nothing catches you off guard.

Your data is not sold, not shared with advertisers, and not used for anything outside of running the app.

Section 01

What Jarvis Can Do

Here's every feature and a plain-English note on what happens with your data when you use it:

AI Chat
Your messages are sent to Groq's API (LLaMA model) to generate replies. Chat history is saved to the database so you can return to previous sessions.
AI Image Generation
Your prompt is sent to Pollinations.ai to generate an image. The prompt is stored in chat history. The image itself is not stored on our servers — only the URL.
Image Recognition
When you upload an image in chat, it is sent to Groq's API (Llama 4 Scout vision model) for analysis. The image is not stored on our servers — only the AI's text response is saved to your chat history.
Quiz Generator
You upload a PDF — its text is extracted and sent to Groq's API to generate multiple-choice questions. The PDF is not stored permanently on our servers.
Model Exam Generator
Similar to the Quiz Generator — your PDF text is sent to Groq to produce a structured exam paper with questions, options, answers, and explanations.
Calculator
Math expressions are evaluated securely and saved to your calculation history in the database.
Time & Date
Current time and date are read from the server (IST timezone) and returned to you. Nothing additional is stored.
AI Image Editing
When you upload an image and ask Jarvis to edit it, the image is sent to NVIDIA's API (Qwen Image Edit model). You can also upload a second image as a mask. Images are not stored on our servers — only the edited result or a compact placeholder is saved to chat history.
Chat Sharing
You can generate a public share link for any chat session. A cleaned copy of the conversation (stripped of internal markers) is stored in the database and accessible to anyone with the link — no login required. Do not share sensitive conversations.
Chat Pinning
You can pin important chat sessions so they appear at the top of your history. The pinned status is stored in the database alongside your chat session.
User Behaviour Profile
Jarvis automatically builds a profile from your chat activity (message frequency, topics, usage patterns). Every 15 messages, this data is sent to Groq's API to generate a personalised summary. The profile is stored in the database and used to make Jarvis's responses more relevant to you. You can clear it from your account page.
Account Management
From the account page you can change your username or password, view and manage saved chats, clear your profile, and permanently delete your account and all associated data.
Section 02

Admin Access to Your Data

Jarvis AI is built and run by Aadin. The admin panel shows stats — visit counts, active users, message counts, and AI-generated profile summaries. Chat messages themselves live in the database and aren't browsed directly.

That said, the data is there. Don't put anything in a chat you'd be uncomfortable with the developer potentially seeing. It's a personal project, not a sealed vault.

If something gets flagged — abusive language, an attempt to extract API keys, stuff like that — an automated alert fires and sends your username and the flagged message to a private Discord channel. That's the main time a specific message gets looked at.

Ban system: The admin can ban individual users or IP addresses. Banned accounts are locked out with a reason shown.

Push notifications: The admin can send push notifications to any user. They show up as in-app alerts the next time your session is active.

Section 03

Sessions & Login Persistence

When you log in, your session is remembered for 30 days. You will stay logged in across browser restarts without needing to sign in again, until you manually log out or the session expires.

Your last active time (heartbeat) is periodically recorded while you use the app. This helps track active user counts in the admin panel.

Section 04

What We Collect

Here's a full list of what's stored when you use Jarvis. Nothing hidden, nothing extra.

  • UsernameThe name you choose at signup. No real name, email, or phone number is ever collected.
  • PasswordStored as a secure hash — your actual password is never saved anywhere.
  • Chat historyYour messages and Jarvis's replies, organised into sessions so you can come back to them.
  • Calculation historyMath expressions you calculate, saved and reviewable from the chat interface.
  • Visit logsAnonymous timestamps when the app is loaded. No IP address or device info is stored.
  • Session activityYour last-active time is periodically recorded to track active users in the admin panel.
  • PDF content (temporary)When using Quiz or Exam features, your PDF text is extracted and sent to Groq's API. It is not stored permanently on our servers.
  • Image promptsWhen you generate an image, the text prompt is saved in your chat history like any other message.
  • Uploaded images (temporary)Images you upload for recognition or editing are sent to Groq's or NVIDIA's API respectively. The images themselves are never stored on our servers — only the AI's response or a compact reference is saved.
  • User behaviour profileA light profile built from your chat activity — topics, message patterns, and an AI-generated summary — used to personalise Jarvis's responses. You can clear this anytime from your account page.
  • Shared chat snapshotsWhen you choose to share a chat, a cleaned copy is stored with a unique link. Anyone with that link can read it.
  • Pinned chat flagsWhether a saved session is pinned is stored alongside your chat history.

User Behaviour Profile

Jarvis automatically builds a profile of your chat activity to personalise its responses. This profile is stored in the database and includes your message counts, average message length, topics of interest, peak usage hour, and a periodic AI-generated summary of your chat style.

Every 15 messages, a sample of your activity is sent to Groq's API to generate a personalised summary and sentiment analysis. This data is used only to improve how Jarvis responds to you.

  • Stored in the databaseYour profile is stored against your username and is visible to the admin.
  • Used to personalise responsesJarvis uses your profile (interests, summary, tone) to tailor its replies in conversation.
  • You can clear itYou can reset your profile at any time from the account page. This will clear all stored interests, summaries, and message statistics.
  • Not shared externallyYour profile data is not shared with any third party beyond what is sent to Groq for the periodic summary generation.
